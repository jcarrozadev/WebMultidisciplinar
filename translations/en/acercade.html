About the Multidisciplinary Project

This project was created by the 1stDAW group of the Virgen de Guadalupe School together with the 2ndDAW students of San José School.
It is created with the purpose of encouraging the students to know how to work together and to know how to manage possible problems and the project itself.
In this way, it also encourages a more realistic way of working and the ability to be self-sufficient and decisive in the face of possible problems that may arise while performing the work.

Assumption 6 - Buffet of Advocacy

Our working assumption for this project is 6.
It is about a law firm, which will have its own software (Application created by 2ndDAW Colegio San José) and the 1stDAW group (Group 2 formed by Javier Arias, Ceus Martin, Mauricio Peña and David Fariña). of the Virgen de Guadalupe School, will be in charge of creating the Database (Entity/Relationship Models) and Web Page.
