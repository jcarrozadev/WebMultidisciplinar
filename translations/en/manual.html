User's Manual - Legal Case Management Application

Welcome to our Legal Case Management Application!
This platform is designed to facilitate lawyers and clients to access relevant information about legal cases using their ID and password.

Access to the Application

Register :
Attorneys must register using their personal information and professional credentials. Clients may register by providing personal details and their relationship to the case.

Login :
Once registered, access the application by entering your ID and password on the login page.

Language change :
In the login section we have a tab to change the language to English/Spanish.
